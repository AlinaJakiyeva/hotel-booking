Our goals

We are students of Astana Aichi University and as the topic of our project work, we chose the creation of a website that could help promote tourism in Kazakhstan in the future. Our team decided to create a website for hotel booking. This is a promising topic, given that many people find it difficult to search for hotels individually. Our site will provide all the necessary services for tourists and will be an excellent travel assistant. We hope that our project will be able to help people and make it easier to visit our country.
