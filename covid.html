General recommendations for travelers



After you've familiarized yourself with the health and cleanliness of the property and the reviews from other guests, we recommend that you do the following:




Check travel restrictions

Please read the travel restrictions for your country and destination before booking. Do this even for those countries that you will be passing through while traveling.


Update your list of things to take on your trip

Don't forget face masks, hand sanitizer, disinfectant wipes, and a thermometer.


Learn about the coronavirus (COVID-19) situation in the country you are traveling to

Before traveling, find out how the coronavirus is doing in the destination country and if there are special rules (for example, visiting public places). It is also important to learn how you can use local health care services when needed. If you have any questions, you can discuss them with the property staff when you arrive.


Keep your distance

When traveling, please try to check-in online and not pay in cash. Observe the distance recommended by local authorities and WHO.


Stay up to date

Be aware of travel restrictions and local government recommendations - they may change during your trip.


Monitor your health

If you discover symptoms of coronavirus during your trip, isolate yourself, inform the property and contact your local healthcare provider..








What is the cancellation policy for coronavirus?


Cancellation may be available due to coronavirus. It depends on several factors: the direction of travel, the date you made the reservation, the date you started the trip, the date of entry, your country of residence and the purpose of the trip.
